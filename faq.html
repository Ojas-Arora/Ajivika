Frequently Asked Questions

What is Ajivika, and how can it help me?

Ajivika is a smart job recommendation portal designed to help job seekers. It lets you create professional resumes, get personalized job suggestions, find skill-boosting courses, and track your career progress easily.

How does Ajivika recommend jobs?

Ajivika analyzes your profile, skills, and preferences to suggest jobs that match your qualifications and interests. It ensures you get relevant and personalized job recommendations.

Can Ajivika help me improve my skills?

Yes! Ajivika suggests courses based on your goals and job interests. You can also take skill assessments to validate your abilities and earn certificates.

Is there support if I have career-related questions?

Absolutely! Ajivika has a chatbot to provide instant help for any career-related queries, making your journey smoother.

Does Ajivika only focus on big cities for job opportunities?

No! Ajivika also highlights jobs in smaller towns and regions, ensuring job seekers from all areas can find opportunities close to home.

How can I create a professional resume using Ajivika?

Ajivika offers an intuitive resume builder with professional templates. Simply fill in your details, select a template, and our AI-powered system will help optimize your resume with industry-specific keywords and formatting.

What types of skill assessments are available?

We offer technical assessments for programming languages, soft skills evaluations, domain-specific tests for various industries, and personality assessments to help you find the perfect career fit.

How does Ajivika ensure job recommendations are relevant?

Our AI algorithm analyzes multiple factors including your skills, experience, location preferences, and career goals. We also consider market trends and employer requirements to provide the most relevant job matches.

Are the courses on Ajivika free?

Ajivika offers both free and premium courses. While many basic courses are available for free, premium courses offer additional features like certificates, mentor support, and detailed assessments.

Can employers post jobs on Ajivika?

Yes! Employers can create accounts to post job openings, search for candidates, and use our AI-powered matching system to find the best talent for their organizations.
